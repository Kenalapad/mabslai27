    Our Vision

    To promote and nurture the habit of savings among the Officers, Enlisted Personnel, Widows of active and retired PAF military personnel and Civilian Employees of the Philippine Air Force (PAF), as well as the members of the families of the aforesaid personnel, who are assigned in Brigadier General Benito N Ebuen Air Base, Lapu Lapu City.... Likewise, to provide a competitive and easily accesible financial products and services with the end in view of delivering the highest returns on savings and contribution of all members.

    Our Mission

    Through the best efforts and leadership of the Board of Trustees with the full support of all the members, MABSLAI shall vigorously strive to:

    Be one of the top movers of making frugality and savings habit of the majority of the Philippine Air Force (PAF) personnel assigned at Brigadier General Benito...N Ebuen Air Base, Lapu-Lapu City;

    Consistently promote and maintain the highest standards of service and good corporate governace to earn the utmost respect of all the members that will drive them to participate in all of its activities and to regularly increase their savings and contributions;

    Strictly comply with the MORNBFI (Manual of Regulations for Non-Bank Financial Institutios) of the BSP and complete adherence with all laws and regulations governing the activities of the association.
